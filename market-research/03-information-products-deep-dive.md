# Information Products + Faceless Content Deep Dive

Expansion of idea #3 from `02-five-ideas-ranked.md`. Micah's call: "information is probably the number 1 way to make money."

## Why this is right (2026 data)

- Digital info products run **90%+ margin** after build.
- Faceless content = **38% of all new creator monetization** (up from 12% in 2022 — 217% growth in 3 years).
- TikTok Creator Rewards now pays **$0.40–$1.00 per 1K qualified views** — 20× the old Creator Fund.
- 72% of Gen Z viewers care about content quality over seeing a face. 86% perceive faceless content as more authentic.

## Format ladder (start with one, layer the rest)

| Format | Setup | Hands-off after launch | Year-1 ceiling | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Faceless TikTok (1min+) | Medium | High (batchable) | $1–10K/mo by mo 6–8 | Top-of-funnel attention |
| Faceless YouTube (long-form) | High | High (after model dials in) | $5–50K/mo (slow ramp) | Compounding ad + affiliate |

| Paid newsletter / community | Medium | Medium | $2–25K/mo at scale | Compounds defensibly |

**Critical for Micah:** cohort hits 85–96% completion vs. 10–20% for self-paced and prices 3–5× higher — but it needs you live, which kills it for his driving schedule. **Self-paced + digital products are his lane.**

## Highest-CPM faceless niches in 2026 (cross-source confirmed)

1. **Personal finance** — $15–22 RPM, $0.40–$1.00/1K TikTok views, strong affiliate (brokerages, credit cards)
2. **AI tools / tech** — $15–22 RPM, strongest affiliate ecosystem (tools pay 20–30% recurring)
3. **Business / B2B** — $15–30 RPM, brand deals 2–3× normal rates
4. **True crime** — $8–13 RPM, brutal competition, proven faceless
5. **Education / language learning** — $9–14 RPM, evergreen, products attach naturally

Finance and AI-tools have the strongest **course / digital product attach** — viewers self-select as buyers.

## The compounding stack ($10K+/mo creators all run this)

```
TikTok/Shorts (top of funnel, faceless, AI-produced)
    ↓ link in bio
Free lead magnet (1-page PDF / Notion doc)
    ↓ email opt-in
Newsletter (1 email/week, AI-drafted, you approve)
    ↓ embedded everywhere
$27–$97 digital product or $197 mini-course
    ↓ for the ~3% who want more
$497 flagship self-paced course
    ↓
Affiliate links throughout (DigitalOcean, AI tools, brokerages)
```

Why ~90% hands-off after build:
- TikTok: 1 batch day = 30 videos scheduled
- Newsletter: AI drafts from video transcripts, review on phone
- Products: instant download, no fulfillment
- Affiliate: passive

Photogen slot-in: thumbnails, social graphics, course covers, lead magnet design — all from owned infra.
