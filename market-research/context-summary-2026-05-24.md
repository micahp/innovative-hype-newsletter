## Compliance rules Micah set — wants ZERO FTC/FDA risk

- **Affiliate only for supplements/wellness — do NOT manufacture or private-label.** Manufacturing breaks the $0 constraint and adds FDA exposure.
- **No health/medical claims.** FTC holds affiliates/endorsers liable too. Educate + link only; affiliate disclosure visible; DSHEA disclaimer on any structure/function language.
- **No fabricated personas.** Faceless = fine; paying real UGC creators later = fine; inventing a fake human with AI photos + fake testimony = off the table.
- **Don't conflate cycles.** Melatonin = circadian (daily) rhythm, NOT menstrual (monthly). No "melatonin re-syncs you to the moon" bridge — false and a claims trap.

- **Cycle-syncing is a confirmed booming 2026 wellness trend**; PMS/menstrual supplement market $22.6B→$35B by 2030.
